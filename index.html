Facilities and Price

Explore our facilities and rates below, designed to give you the best value in comfort and experience. For inquiries or custom offers, feel free to reach out through the contact form at the bottom of this page.

Location

Just 10 minutes from Ubud central, our villas is nestled in the peaceful village of Br. Kendran - a hidden gem known for its rich culture and natural beauty. Located within a designated tourism village, it offers the perfect balance of authentic Balinese charm and quiet seclusion.

Bedroom

This spacious 8x8 meter bedroom blends natural charm with refined comfort. Designed with warm tones and artisanal touches, it's the perfect place to unwind and feel at ease.

Nature Vibes

Surrounded by whispering bamboo forest, the villa offers a serene escape where nature takes center stage. Let the rustling leaves and gentle breeze reconnect you with the calm, grounding rhythm of the earth.

Wifi

Stay connected with our high-speed Wi-fi, perfect for remote work. Whatever you're working by the pool or in your villa, productivity meets paradise.

Pool

Your private pool is a secluded oasis, surrounded by lush greenery and the soothing sounds of nature. Whether you're taking a refreshing dip or simply loughing under the sun, it's perfect space to relax, recharge, and indulge in total privacy.

Size

Set on 150 square meters of private space, the villas offers a perfect blend of comfort, privacy, and open-air living. Enjoy the freedom to relax, unwind, and feel completely at home in your own.

Keamanan

Your safety and peace of mind are our priority, with the 24-hour security ensuring a worry-free stay. Relax and enjoy every moments, knowing you're in trusted hands.
